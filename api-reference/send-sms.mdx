---
title: "Send SMS"
openapi: "POST /api/services/sendsms/"
---

Submits one SMS to one recipient. Returns immediately with a
`messageid` you can use later to look up the delivery state via
[Get delivery report](/api-reference/get-delivery-report).

## Field notes

- **`mobile`** — international format, no `+`. Kenyan numbers start
  with `254`. Example: `254712345678`.
- **`shortcode`** — your approved sender ID. Use `XYRA` for test traffic.
  See [Sender IDs](/concepts/sender-ids) for registration.
- **`message`** — up to 1530 characters. See [Encoding](/concepts/encoding)
  for how length translates to billed segments.
- **`pass_type`** — always `"plain"`. Reserved for future encrypted modes.
- **`clientsmsid`** *(optional)* — an integer of your choosing,
  echoed back in the response. Use it to correlate the response with
  your own records when you don't want to depend on `messageid`.

## What "success" means here

A `200` response with `response-description: "Success"` means the
message has been **accepted for delivery** — not that the handset has
received it. Use [Get delivery report](/api-reference/get-delivery-report)
to confirm final delivery.
